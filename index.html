About me

Hello and welcome!

I am a passionate game and full-stack developer who specialises in game design and programming.

I particularly enjoy coming up with new game ideas, turning them into prototypes and games, and learning about new technologies in the process. I am fascinated by developing innovative and previously unknown game mechanics. For my bachelor's thesis, I created a prototype that included a time travel mechanic: the player's actions were recorded in the game world during runtime and replayed at a later point in the game.

In addition, I always try to incorporate social aspects into my games. The player should learn something beyond the purely playful. This was also evident in my recently completed master's thesis, in which I investigated how asymmetric multiplayer can improve communication between players when solving puzzles together.

I recently completed my master's degree at Furtwangen University (HFU), where I had previously completed my bachelor's degree. During my studies, I gained valuable experience in computer science and game development. In addition, through my work as a student trainee at asellerate GmbH, I have acquired in-depth knowledge of web development and related aspects.

My preferred game engine is Unity, but I also have experience with other engines such as Unreal and Godot. I am also proficient in several programming languages, including C#, Java, Python, JavaScript and TypeScript. I am also particularly familiar with the development of web applications based on Vue and Nuxt.

If you would like to learn more about my work, please take a look at my portfolio or contact me directly to discuss potential projects or job offers.

I look forward to working with other passionate developers to create games that entertain, challenge and inspire players of all ages.
